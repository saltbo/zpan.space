Self-hosting for yourself and operating a file service for other people are different jobs. ZPan 2.6 introduced the controls needed for the second one while keeping the Community edition useful on its own.

## Community, Pro, and Business

Community covers the core file workflows and remains open source. Pro adds controls for professional operators, including licensing, branding, white-label presentation, auditing, and higher operational limits. Business builds on that foundation with quota-store and commercial capabilities.

## Cloud-managed licenses, self-hosted data

A deployment can bind a Pro or Business license through ZPan Cloud. The license controls entitlements; it does not relocate stored files. Operators continue to choose their application deployment and S3 provider.

## Better operations inside the product

Audit trails, announcements, richer previews, and quota foundations make a deployment easier to govern as its user base grows. The release separates core file capability from operator tooling without creating a separate application.

Announcements are for maintenance windows, service changes, and important release notices. Keep durable instructions in documentation and link to them from the announcement.

Audit Logs explain changes to users, teams, storage, settings, and administrative resources. Filter by time and resource before inspecting actor and metadata. External bucket changes, cloud permissions, and proxy configuration require logs from those systems too.

Analytics summarizes growth, storage, traffic, sharing, and operations. Empty charts on a new site do not necessarily indicate failure; some history appears after scheduled rollups run.

Verify sign-in, upload, download, sharing, email, and WebDAV after upgrades. Review failed jobs, downloader heartbeat, storage capacity, and unusual traffic weekly; audit administrators, team owners, manual quota grants, secrets, and backup recovery monthly.
